Introduction

Currently, I am Ph.D. Student at Hanyang University under the supervision of Professor Younghoon Kim. My main research areas are natural language processing, large language models, model interpretation, and interactive AI. I specialize in two areas: (1) Discovering the underlying behaviors of deep learning models for better understanding and (2) Developing interactive deep learning models with human feedback, such as text prompts and clicks.
